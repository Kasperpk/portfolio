How I work

What you can expect when we work together

I build the data infrastructure that turns raw data into trustworthy information your team can actually act on. I'm certified on Databricks and Microsoft Fabric, so there's no ramp-up — I plug in and start contributing to the work you're already prioritizing. I write clean, modular PySpark and SQL, shipped through proper CI/CD with YAML and asset bundles, and model your business with dimensional design — SCD2, surrogate keys, bridge tables — so analysts and developers finally share one picture of how the company actually works.

I also have opinions. When I see an architecture choice that will hurt you in twelve months, I'll say so — kindly, but clearly. You're not paying me to nod.

The work starts before the code. I use design thinking to dig into what you actually need, not what's easiest to build, because solutions built on guesses don't survive contact with reality. And while we're working together, you'll never wonder where things stand: short updates, plain language, quick replies. If something will take a few days, I'll tell you it'll take a few days — not let you find out by chasing me.

That's the deal: serious technical work, honest opinions, clear communication, and a person who's genuinely good to have on the team.
